Features

Deduplicate Using AI

Train the model with your own enterprise data. SiNGL uses advanced algorithms and creates the scores for each record and decides whether 2 records belong to the same physical entity or not depending on the thresholds set.

Outcome: Obtain accurate deduplication

Advanced Data Stewardship

The input data is categorised into 3 buckets - Unique, Duplicate and Suspects - Using the intuitive Data Stewardship UI, you can review the duplicates and suspect and make final decision to survive the Unique record.

Outcome: Perform stewardship in few simple steps

Bulk Match

You have thousands of suspect records and don't have the time or stewards to manually verify every record. Use this option to perform bulk match and merge in minutes.

Outcome: Stewardship in minutes - Not days

Customer 360°

You can now view your customer and their relationship with other customers, products they subscribe to and accounts. You can also view the households and increase your marketing ROI

Outcome: Bring out hidden relationships in your data

Business Outcome: Upsell, Cross-sell and Increase your marketing ROI

Consume data via API

Your onboarding systems and consuming applications can check for the existence of the customer in real-time before a new customer is created.

Outcome: Improve Data Quality by preventing duplicate data
Business Outcome: Faster, Lower cost of onboarding

GenAI Powered Data Stewardship (Chat with your data in Natural Language)

You can now ask any question to the dataset - look for data anomalies, filter data based on some criteria, find total number of records etc.

Outcome: Gain deep understanding of your customer data

Search Golden Record

Look for customer records based on a variety of search criteria.

Outcome: Golden Record in days and weeks - Not months
